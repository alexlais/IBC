Gold Programs

Gold is an excellent store of value. Historically, currencies have risen and fallen. Central banks and governments may debase currencies but they cannot dilute bullion. Governments often discourage gold ownership with high taxes, control regulations and "jawboning". Gold price fluctuations may depend on multiple factors such as global strife, confidence in financial markets, governments or banks, currency positioning maneuvers, etc. Over the centuries gold's long term value has risen rather steadily! IBC's team of analysts provides periodic reports that help our clients to construct their portfolio strategy. IBC coordinates client gold positions in collaboration with the most secure storage vaults.
